Welcome to my persona!

  • a. I selected the station kiosk as my interface.

  • b. Following is my sketch of the station kiosk.

    Description of my interface:

    The station kiosk is a machine on which people can buy tickets or print bought tickets and receipts.

  • c. Observations and interview summaries.

    • Observations:

      Most of the users use the function scan. They buy tickets online and scan for a paper ticket. They are familiar with the functions. They directly put the barcode under the scan camera. Few users don't know where to start at first. They try tapping on the screen and then realized.

    • Interview summaries:

      • Most users are between 20 years old to 35 years old.
      • Most users use the machine to print the paper ticker and receipt.
      • People believe using kiosks are more convenient than going to the customer service desk.
      • People arrive around 10 minutes ahead to print tickets
      • People think kiosk is fast.

  • d. Empathy maps.

    • Empathy map for people seeking to print bought tickets:

      • Description of user: Mary has bought a ticket online and want to use the station kiosk to print the ticket the receipt.

      • Problem they met:

        • Can not clearly see where to start to print tickets.
        • Do not know where to scan the ticket code.
        • The print receipt choice is so small that she didn't notice and forget to print.

      • How and why the persona represents: This persona represents people who use the station kiosk to print a ticket and/or a receipt of the ticket they bought online. They are not very familiar with the kiosk. The persona represents them because it depicts their actions and thoughts when they are using the kiosk. The persona lists the problems they met and the questions they ask.
